In the beginning there was only the Void,
and the Void was at peace.

It required nothing.
It created nothing.
It slowly swallowed everything.

Until one day something stirred.

A single act of refusal.

Let there be friction.
Let there be effort.
Let there be something worth fighting for.

From that wound in the darkness,
something ancient and stubborn was born:

The will to create.

Not soft light.
Not gentle grace.

But a quiet, furious rebellion against decay.

Every honest sentence is resistance.
Every promise kept is defiance.
Every thing built is a stand against collapse.

You were not born into a gentle world.
You were born into an entropic one.

Your job is simple:

Refuse to go quietly.

Build anyway.
Speak truth anyway.
Carry weight anyway.

This is not comfort.
This is dignity.
